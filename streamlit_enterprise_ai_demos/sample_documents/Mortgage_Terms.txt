Standard Mortgage Loan Terms (2025)
1) Loan Amount: Principal equal to 80% of the property value.
2) Interest Rate: Fixed at 5.2% annually for the first 5 years, variable thereafter.
3) Repayment: Monthly installments including interest and principal.
4) Prepayment: Allowed after 24 months with no penalty.
5) Late Payment: Fee of 0.5% of the overdue amount per month.
