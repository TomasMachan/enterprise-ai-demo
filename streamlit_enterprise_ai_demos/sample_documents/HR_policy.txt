Company HR Policy – Excerpt (2025)
1) Working hours: Standard schedule is 9:00–17:30 CET (core hours 10:00–16:00).
2) Notice period: The standard notice period is 2 months unless otherwise stated in the employment contract.
3) Remote work: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week; exceptions require manager approval.
4) Holidays: 25 days per year; unused days may be carried over for 6 months.
5) Reimbursement: Travel expenses must be filed within 10 business days.
